# Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** March 13, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 13, 2026

This Refund Policy explains the terms governing refunds for purchases made on the XURL URL shortening platform ("Service").

## 1. Billing Model

XURL operates on a one-time purchase model. Paid plans are not subscriptions and do not auto-renew. Each purchase adds a fixed number of link creation credits to your cumulative quota.

### Plan Pricing

| Plan | Price (INR) | Links Added |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Starter | 49 | 5 |
| Pro | 99 | 25 |
| Business | 199 | 100 |
| Enterprise | 299 | 300 |
| Big Enterprise | 999 | 600 |

All prices are in Indian Rupees (INR) and are inclusive of applicable taxes.

## 2. Cumulative Quota

Purchased link credits are added to your cumulative quota permanently. Quotas do not expire and are not reset. Purchasing the same plan multiple times adds additional credits each time.

## 3. Refund Eligibility

### Eligible for Refund

You may be eligible for a refund if:

- **Technical failure** — Your payment was processed but the quota was not applied to your account due to a system error, and the issue cannot be resolved by our support team.
- **Duplicate charge** — You were charged multiple times for the same transaction unintentionally, and the duplicate charges are verified in our payment records.
- **Service unavailability** — The Service was completely unavailable for an extended period immediately following your purchase, preventing you from using any of your purchased credits.

### Not Eligible for Refund

Refunds will not be issued for:

- **Used quota** — Link credits that have already been used to create shortened URLs.
- **Change of mind** — Deciding you no longer need the Service after purchase.
- **Plan dissatisfaction** — Purchasing a plan and then preferring a different tier. You can purchase an additional plan at any time to add more credits.
- **Account termination for cause** — Accounts terminated for violating our [Terms of Service](Terms-of-Service.md) or [Acceptable Use Policy](Acceptable-Use-Policy.md).
- **Expired links** — Links that have reached their TTL expiry. Link expiry is a core feature of each plan tier and is disclosed at the time of purchase.

## 4. Refund Process

### Requesting a Refund

To request a refund:

1. Contact us through the information provided on our website within 7 days of your purchase.
2. Include your registered email address and the transaction/order ID.
3. Describe the reason for your refund request with any relevant details.

### Processing

- Refund requests are reviewed within 5 business days.
- Approved refunds are processed through Razorpay to your original payment method.
- Refund processing times depend on your bank or payment provider and typically take 5-10 business days after approval.

### Partial Refunds

If you have used some of the credits from your purchase, a partial refund may be issued for the unused portion at our discretion.

## 5. Cancellation

### Free Plan

The free plan does not involve any payment and can be discontinued at any time by simply not using the Service.

### Paid Plans

Since paid plans are one-time purchases (not subscriptions), there is no recurring billing to cancel. Your purchased credits remain available until used.

### Account Deletion

If you request account deletion:

- Any unused quota will be forfeited.
- Active shortened links will be deactivated.
- This action is irreversible and does not qualify for a refund.

## 6. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank or payment provider without first contacting us for resolution, we reserve the right to:

- Immediately suspend your account
- Forfeit any remaining quota
- Restrict future access to the Service

We encourage you to contact us first to resolve any billing disputes amicably.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the Service. The policy in effect at the time of your purchase governs that transaction.

## 8. Contact

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please reach out through the contact information provided on our website.
