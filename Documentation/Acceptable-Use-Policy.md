# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** March 13, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 13, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the rules and guidelines for using the XURL URL shortening platform ("Service"). By using the Service, you agree to comply with this policy. Violations may result in immediate suspension or termination of your account without prior notice or refund.

## 1. Prohibited Content

You must not use the Service to create shortened links that redirect to or promote:

### Malware and Phishing

- Websites that distribute viruses, trojans, ransomware, spyware, or other malicious software
- Pages designed to steal user credentials, financial information, or personal data
- Drive-by download pages or exploit kits
- Fake login pages that mimic legitimate services

### Spam and Deceptive Content

- Unsolicited bulk messaging or promotional campaigns
- Misleading redirects that disguise the true destination
- Click fraud schemes or artificial traffic generation
- Chain letters, pyramid schemes, or multi-level marketing scams

### Illegal Content

- Content that violates any applicable local, national, or international law
- Material that infringes copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property rights
- Content related to illegal drugs, weapons trafficking, or human trafficking
- Child exploitation material of any kind

### Hate Speech and Harassment

- Content that promotes violence against individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or national origin
- Targeted harassment, bullying, or threats against individuals
- Content that glorifies or incites terrorism

### Adult Content

- Links to pornographic material without appropriate context or consent
- Non-consensual intimate imagery

## 2. Prohibited Activities

You must not:

- **Circumvent protections** — Attempt to bypass rate limits, abuse detection, quota enforcement, or any other security measures
- **Scan or enumerate** — Conduct automated scanning, brute-forcing, or enumeration of shortened URLs
- **Abuse guest access** — Use multiple identities, VPNs, or proxies to circumvent guest usage limits
- **Impersonate** — Create links that impersonate or falsely represent XURL, its staff, or other users
- **Interfere with service** — Attempt to overload, crash, or otherwise disrupt the availability of the Service
- **Reverse engineer** — Decompile, disassemble, or reverse-engineer any component of the Service
- **Automate without authorization** — Use bots, scrapers, or automated tools to interact with the Service without explicit permission
- **Mask malicious intent** — Use URL shortening to disguise phishing, malware, or scam links

## 3. Spam Restrictions

The following activities constitute spam and are strictly prohibited:

- Creating shortened links for the purpose of bulk unsolicited messaging across any platform (email, social media, messaging apps)
- Using the Service to inflate engagement metrics or manipulate social media algorithms
- Generating links at high volume with the intent to distribute them in automated or semi-automated campaigns
- Creating links designed to redirect through multiple URL shorteners to obscure the final destination

## 4. Abuse Enforcement

### Automated Detection

XURL employs automated systems to detect and prevent abuse:

- **Abuse scoring** — Behavioral analysis assigns risk scores to requests; high-scoring requests are blocked automatically
- **Rate limiting** — Requests exceeding defined thresholds are throttled or rejected
- **Negative caching** — Anti-scan defense blocks repeated lookups of non-existent slugs
- **URL validation** — Submitted URLs are checked against known malicious patterns and SSRF protections

### Manual Review

We may conduct manual reviews of flagged accounts and links. Links identified as violating this policy may be disabled without prior notice.

### Enforcement Actions

Depending on the severity and nature of the violation, we may take one or more of the following actions:

1. **Warning** — Notification of the violation with a request to cease the behavior
2. **Link Disabling** — Immediate deactivation of the offending shortened URL(s)
3. **Account Suspension** — Temporary restriction of account access pending review
4. **Account Termination** — Permanent closure of the account with forfeiture of any remaining quota
5. **Legal Action** — Referral to law enforcement for violations involving illegal activity

### No Refunds for Violations

Users whose accounts are terminated for policy violations are not entitled to refunds for any purchased plans or remaining quota.

## 5. Reporting Abuse

If you encounter a shortened link that violates this policy, please report it through the contact information provided on our website. Include:

- The shortened URL in question
- A description of the violation
- Any supporting evidence

We review all reports and take appropriate action promptly.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Acceptable Use Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the Service. Your continued use of the Service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## 7. Contact

For questions about this Acceptable Use Policy, please reach out through the contact information provided on our website.
